    Rules & Regulations






    DO'S DONT'S
    Always be in queue and wait for your turn. Do not accept bribe in favour of your vote. Bribery is an offence.
    Always maintain peace in and around the polling station. Do not impersonate for someone else. Impersonationis an offence.
    Show your photo ID card as prescribed by the Election Commission of India as in Panel No. 4 Do not cause any disturbance in the poll process. You may land up in prison.
    Follow instructions on "HOW TO VOTE" Do not cause damage/tamper with poll mateial including EVM/VVPAT. You may land up in prison.
    Show courtesy to the polling team which is facilitation your vote. Do not obstruct the polling team in performing election duty. You may land up in prison.
    After casting your vote come out of the polling station quietly. Do not litter/spit in and around the polling station. It is an offence.
